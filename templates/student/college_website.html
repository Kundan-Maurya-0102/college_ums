About the Institute
Building Technical Excellence Since 2016
Government Polytechnic Tekari was established in July 2016 under the Department of Science and Technology, Government of Bihar. Approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and affiliated to the State Board of Technical Education (SBTE), Bihar, the institute is dedicated to providing quality diploma-level technical education to students from the region.

The institute fosters technical excellence and skill development to prepare students for the demands of modern industry and society. With a strong emphasis on practical training, government-supported infrastructure, and experienced faculty, GP Tekari stands as a beacon of accessible engineering education in Gaya district.
